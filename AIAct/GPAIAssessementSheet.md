# EU AI Act Assessment: Role Determination for GPAI

This diagnostic helps determine whether your organization acts as a **Provider** or a **Deployer** for a specific AI system or General-Purpose AI (GPAI) model integration.

| # | Assessment Question | If YES, you are: | If NO, you are: |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **1** | Is the AI system being used solely for professional activities under your authority (e.g., internal CRM analytics)? | **Deployer** | **Provider** |
| **2** | Did your team develop the underlying GPAI model architecture or hire a contractor to build it specifically for you? | **Provider** | **Deployer** |
| **3** | Is your primary role to ensure the system is used according to the "Instructions for Use" provided by a vendor? | **Deployer** | **Provider** |
| **4** | Have you placed your own trademark or brand name on the AI product interface before releasing it to subsidiaries? | **Provider** | **Deployer** |
| **5** | Is your main responsibility to monitor the system for "drifts" or errors and report them to the developer? | **Deployer** | **Provider** |
| **6** | Have you modified a non-high-risk GPAI tool to perform a "High-Risk" task (e.g., recruitment screening)? | **Provider** | **Deployer** |
| **7** | Do you exercise control over the core training data and model weights rather than just the input prompts? | **Provider** | **Deployer** |
| **8** | Is your duty limited to providing human oversight for the system's outputs based on a manual? | **Deployer** | **Provider** |
| **9** | Have you made "Substantial Modifications" to the code that change the system's original safety parameters? | **Provider** | **Deployer** |
| **10** | Do you only manage the "Input Data" (customer files/prompts) that are fed into someone elses API? | **Deployer** | **Provider** |
| **11** | Are you responsible for drafting the "Technical Documentation" (Annex IV) and the EU Declaration of Conformity? | **Provider** | **Deployer** |
| **12** | Does your organization solely consume the results of the AI (e.g., recommendations) to drive business logic? | **Deployer** | **Provider** |
| **13** | Did you create the Quality Management System (QMS) and post-market monitoring plan for this AI system? | **Provider** | **Deployer** |
| **14** | Is the AI system an "off-the-shelf" component you integrated into your existing platform without changing its logic? | **Deployer** | **Provider** |
| **15** | Are you legally required to publish the public summary of training data (as per Article 53 for GPAI)? | **Provider** | **Deployer** |
| **16** | Is the system integrated into a retail product you sell to customers under your brand's name? | **Provider** | **Deployer** |
| **17** | Do you only have control over "operational settings" like confidence thresholds or filtering toggles? | **Deployer** | **Provider** |
| **18** | Are you acting as the "Authorised Representative" for a developer located outside of the European Union? | **Provider** | **Deployer** |
| **19** | Is it your job to perform a "Fundamental Rights Impact Assessment" (FRIA) for a high-risk system you use? | **Deployer** | **Provider** |
| **20** | Do you hold the CE marking responsibility and perform the official Conformity Assessment? | **Provider** | **Deployer** |
